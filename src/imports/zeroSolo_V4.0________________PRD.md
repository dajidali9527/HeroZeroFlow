# 一、项目定位

zeroSolo 不是传统软件公司官网，也不是单纯 AI 产品展示页。

它是：

> 一个展示如何从现实问题出发，通过理解、组合 AI 与数字技术，最终形成可运行系统的品牌空间。

当前阶段目标：

1. 建立强烈第一印象
2. 展示设计能力与技术整合能力
3. 展示真实探索方向和已验证 Demo
4. 为未来商业合作建立信任基础

核心表达：

> 把复杂的问题，转化为可运行的系统。

# 二十、Figma Prompt

Design the official website experience for zeroSolo (Chinese brand: 零维破界).

Create a continuous visual metaphor called "Zero Flow".

The entire website should feel like one flowing river:

starting from chaotic real-world problems and technology fragments,

converging into zeroSolo,

transforming into practical digital systems,

passing through capability modules,

and continuing into future exploration.

The hero section should not be a typical AI landing page.

Use:

- three continuous flowing lines as river structure
- broken curves as exploration paths
- larger readable floating fragments
- Chinese-friendly typography

The visual story:

Chaos → Understanding → Reorganization → Systems → Future

Core message:

把复杂的问题，转化为可运行的系统。
